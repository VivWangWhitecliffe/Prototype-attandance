Here are some key explanations of the design:

Faculty Interface

  • Attendance Tab: Lecturers can use this tab to complete attendance sheets and modify a student's final status.
  • At-Risk Students: Lecturers have the ability to review students labeled as "at risk," add comments, and transition them to the "on track" status.
  • Student Details Page: Lecturers can change student details, add or delete students from specific courses. Course and Student Selection: When a course and a student are selected, lecturers can delete the chosen student, modify details, or add a new student.
